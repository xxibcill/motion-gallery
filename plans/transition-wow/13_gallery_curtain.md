# Task 13: Gallery Curtain Demo

## Status
Done

## Goal
Build a lush, theatrical curtain transition with layered fabric-like sweeps, gradient veils, and refined reveal timing.

## Route
- `/transition/gallery-curtain`

## Deliverables
- A premium curtain-style transition with strong art direction.
- Layered veils or drapes with believable sequence and weight.
- A palette and type treatment that feels luxurious rather than generic.

## Files And Surfaces
- `app/transition/gallery-curtain/page.tsx`
- `components/transition-lab/GradientVeil.tsx`
- Optional texture or noise overlays
- `lib/animation-registry.ts`

## Dependencies
- Tasks 01 through 03

## Steps
1. Build multiple sweeping veil layers rather than a single wipe.
2. Use easing and stagger to imply fabric weight and drag.
3. Add texture, grain, or low-contrast lighting to avoid flat gradients.
4. Reveal the target scene in a controlled sequence.

## Acceptance Criteria
- The transition feels theatrical and premium.
- Layering creates believable depth and weight.
- The page does not collapse into a standard full-screen wipe.
- The route adds a softer but still dramatic flavor to the lab.
