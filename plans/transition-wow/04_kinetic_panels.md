# Task 04: Kinetic Panels Demo

## Status
Done

## Goal
Create a bold editorial transition where oversized content slabs slam in from different directions with staggered depth and hard contrast.

## Route
- `/transition/kinetic-panels`

## Deliverables
- A demo page that feels like a fashion editorial or poster wall.
- Panel-based transitions with strong directional movement.
- Distinct typography and asymmetrical composition.

## Files And Surfaces
- `app/transition/kinetic-panels/page.tsx`
- `components/transition-lab/*` shared pieces as needed
- `lib/animation-registry.ts`

## Dependencies
- Tasks 01 through 03

## Steps
1. Build a scene with multiple stacked panels that enter from separate axes.
2. Use overshoot and stagger to make the movement feel aggressive and intentional.
3. Pair the motion with striking typography and sharp grid alignment.
4. Add one interaction that replays or scrubs the transition.

## Acceptance Criteria
- The effect feels forceful and graphic, not soft or generic.
- The layout remains readable while panels animate.
- The transition is clearly different from the reveal and text-reveal pages.
- The route is discoverable from `/transition`.
