# Task 12: Void Portal Demo

## Status
Done

## Goal
Create a dramatic portal or vortex transition where the current scene is consumed into a central void and the next scene emerges from the same aperture.

## Route
- `/transition/void-portal`

## Deliverables
- A portal-based transition with a strong center-of-gravity.
- Tunnel, vortex, or radial-collapse visual language.
- A dark, immersive atmosphere with controlled light accents.

## Files And Surfaces
- `app/transition/void-portal/page.tsx`
- Optional radial mask or vortex helpers
- `lib/animation-registry.ts`

## Dependencies
- Tasks 01 through 03

## Steps
1. Build a radial transition system anchored to a central portal.
2. Pull or scale the current scene toward the portal with layered depth.
3. Reveal the next scene through the aperture with a distinct second phase.
4. Use atmospheric lighting to make the portal feel spatial.

## Acceptance Criteria
- The portal reads as a real scene transition, not just a circular mask.
- The two-phase motion of consume and reveal is easy to follow.
- The page delivers a clear wow moment.
- The route remains performant enough for repeated playback.
